Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 October 2025

LiftLog helps you track your training. Your data is stored on your device and, if you choose to sign in, can be synchronized securely via our backend. We do not sell personal data.

Information we collect

  • Training data you enter (sessions, templates, measurements) and app settings. Stored locally on your device and, if signed in, synced to your account.
  • Account information if you sign in: your email address and user ID managed by our authentication provider.
  • Purchase information if you buy subscriptions or in‑app products via Google Play Billing (order identifiers, purchase tokens). Payment is processed by Google; we do not receive your full payment card details.
  • Basic technical data for reliability (e.g., app version, platform). We do not use third‑party advertising SDKs or behavioral ad tracking.

How we use your information

  • Provide core app features (logging, charts, offline access).
  • Sync your data across devices if you sign in.
  • Process purchases and manage entitlements.
  • Maintain security, fix bugs, and improve performance.

Sharing and third parties

We share data only as needed to provide the service:

  • Authentication and data sync via Supabase. See their policy for how they handle account data.
  • Purchases via Google Play Billing. Google processes payments and provides purchase tokens and status to us.

We do not share your data with advertisers.

Data storage and retention

  • On‑device data stays on your device unless you sign in to enable sync.
  • Synced data is stored in your Supabase account and retained until you delete it or request deletion.
  • You can delete local data from the app at any time.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your data. Contact us and we’ll assist.

Children’s privacy

LiftLog is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Security

We use industry‑standard measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We’ll update the “Last updated” date and, when appropriate, notify you in‑app.
