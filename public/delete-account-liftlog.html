Delete your LiftLog account and data

Developer: Ciaran Engelbrecht • App: LiftLog • Last updated: 12 October 2025

How to request deletion

  1. Open the LiftLog app and go to Settings → Account.
  2. Tap “Delete account & data”.
  3. Confirm twice. You may be asked to re‑authenticate.

If you cannot access the app, email contact@ciaranengelbrecht.com from your account email and request deletion.

What will be deleted

  • Your LiftLog cloud account (Supabase auth user)
  • Your synced data (sessions, exercises, measurements, templates, settings)
  • Any active LiftLog sessions and tokens

What might be retained

  • Purchase records processed by Google Play Billing are retained by Google and may be kept for tax, fraud, or accounting obligations. We do not store your full payment card details.
  • Server logs and backups may persist for up to 30 days and are purged on a rolling basis.

Local data on your device

LiftLog stores data locally for offline use. During deletion, the app clears local data. If you are unable to sign in to perform the deletion, you can also remove local data via device settings (clear site data / app data) or contact us for help.

Timeline

Account deletion is initiated immediately and typically completes within minutes. Certain retained records (e.g., purchase metadata) may be kept for the periods noted above.
