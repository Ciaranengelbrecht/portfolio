Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

LiftLog helps you track gym sessions, exercise templates, body measurements, and training progress. We do not sell personal data and we do not use third-party advertising SDKs or behavioral ad tracking.

Information we collect

  • Account information if you sign in: your email address, Supabase user ID, and authentication session details.
  • Training data you enter: exercises, sessions, sets, reps, weights, templates, program settings, and workout notes.
  • Measurement data you enter or import: weight, circumferences, skinfold values, body-fat estimates, lean mass, and related body composition fields.
  • App settings and local preferences such as theme, timer, privacy, import/export, and display options.
  • Basic operational diagnostics if enabled, such as app version, platform, startup failures, sync failures, and error details needed to maintain reliability.

How we use information

  • Provide workout logging, charts, templates, and offline access.
  • Sync your data across devices when you sign in.
  • Support backup, export, import, account deletion, and recovery.
  • Maintain security, troubleshoot bugs, and improve performance.

Storage and sync

  • LiftLog stores working data locally on your device for offline use.
  • If you sign in, data is synced to Supabase under your account and protected by row-level security policies.
  • Export files are generated on your device. If you share or store exports elsewhere, that copy is controlled by you.

Sharing and third parties

We share data only as needed to operate the app:

  • Supabase provides authentication, database storage, and sync infrastructure.
  • App stores may process installation, crash, and review metadata according to their own policies.

LiftLog v1 is free and does not use in-app purchases.

Health and fitness information

Body composition, recovery, and measurement estimates are training tools only. They are not medical advice and should not replace guidance from a qualified health professional.

Retention and deletion

  • Synced account data is retained until you delete it or request deletion.
  • You can delete your account and synced data from LiftLog Settings, or request help by email.
  • Local device data can also be removed by clearing app/site data on your device.
  • Server logs and backups may persist briefly for security and reliability before being removed on a rolling basis.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your data. Contact us and we will assist.

Children's privacy

LiftLog is not directed to children under 13 or the minimum age in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Security

We use reasonable technical safeguards, including authenticated sync and database access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.
