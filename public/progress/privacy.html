Privacy Policy

LiftLog stores your training data locally on your device and optionally synchronizes with Supabase if you sign in. We do not sell or share personal information.

Data we store

  • Training sessions, templates, and measurements you enter
  • Settings and preferences
  • If you sign in: minimal account identifiers (email/UID) managed by Supabase

How we use data

  • Provide core app functionality, analytics charts, and offline access
  • Improve reliability and performance

Third parties

We use Supabase for authentication and optional data sync. Their services are subject to their own privacy policy.
